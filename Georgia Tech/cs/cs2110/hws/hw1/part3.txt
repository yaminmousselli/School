1. What is the purpose of a splitter/joiner?

The purpose of a splitter/joiner is to break up an input consisting of bits and break that input into multiple inputs consisting of individual bits. In addition, splitters are non-directional. 

2. Why are tunnels useful?

Tunnels help keep your circuits organized. Instead of having an output circle, you can have a tunnel.


3. What is the output of your circuit given an input of 010? How about an input of 100? 

The output of the circuit given an input of 010 is 0. The output of the circuit given an input of 100 is 1.
